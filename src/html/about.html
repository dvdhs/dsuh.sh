Hi! I'm David Suh, a passionate engineer, programmer, and math enthusiast. A close friend of mine hooked me onto programming in highschool and I haven't stopped since. I was born in 2001 in Austin, Texas, and spent some time there before moving back to South Korea, where I spent much of my formative years. I returned to the US for college, where I attended the University of Chicago from 2020-2024. My areas of interest include, but are not limited to: low level optimizations, SIMD programming, parallel programming, machine learning, networks. My favorite IDE is vim + tmux with plugins.
